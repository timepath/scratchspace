00029 /*
00030 * Uses a entity calld swampslug to handle players in the swamp
00031 * It works like this: When the plyer enters teh swamp the spawnfunc_trigger_swamp
00032 * attaches a new "swampslug" to the player. As long as the plyer is inside
00033 * the swamp the swamp gives the slug new health. But the slug slowly kills itself
00034 * so when the player goes outside the swamp, it dies and releases the player from the
00035 * swamps curses (dmg/slowdown)
00036 *
00037 * I do it this way becuz there is no "untouch" event.
00038 */
